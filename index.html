About Me

I'm Pablo Wunderlich. I'm a games music composer and a technical sound designer. I enjoy designing the sound-scape for games and other visual media from the sound effects and audio design to the musical composition. I love creating audio systems through meticulous implementation be it through scripting into the game engine and or using audio middleware such as FMOD and or Wwise.


Already have a composer? No problem. I can design the sounds for your game and implement them.


Already have a composer and a sound designer? No problem. I can hop into your team and help with the implementation of the sounds into the engine with or without audio middleware.


Whatever your game_audio needs are I can help you reach the sonic-landscape you envisioned to deliver a fully immersible experience.
